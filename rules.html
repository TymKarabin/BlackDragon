Home Rules:

  • 1. Children shall obey their parents.
  • 2. Children shall greet their parents with “Hi Mom! Hi Dad” when they enter the home and say “Goodbye” when they leave.
  • 3. Children will always be respectful of their parents, teachers and elders.
  • 4. Children will not use any Karate moves on their family members or friends. (If found using it on family members or friends or in the schoolyard, in an abusive way, that person will be suspended from training)
  • 5. Children will be kind to their brothers and sisters.
  • 6. Children will keep their hair, body and teeth clean daily.
  • 7. Children must say EXCUSE ME if they interrupt a conversation that is going on.
  • 8. Children must try their BEST to do well in school. They will make sure that their homework is done and study daily.
  • 9. Children should have a positive attitude and not a negative attitude.

Make sure your homework is done on time.

Make sure that you eat a balanced meal every day, to keep your body strong.

CHILDREN WHO DO NOT OBEY THEIR PARENTS CHEERFULLY MAY BE REDUCED IN RANK.
