About Us

At Black Dragon, we pride ourselves on our excellent instructors and our champion student. With outstanding instruction, our students have the ability to face all kinds of adversity and life situations. Parents will attest to significant changes in their children. Our students are taught respect in and out of our studio. Their marks have increased through self-confidence and the ability to focus more. Teaching your children how to defend themselves in a situation these days is very important. More and more we learn about Junior high and High School children carrying knives and guns to school. Our students are taught the difference between bullying and defending themselves.

At Black Dragon, we will teach your child street self defense, confidence and respect. Self Defense is more than kicks, punches and blocks. Timing, distancing, environment and mindset are just some of the things to consider.

Along with self-defense, we will teach you Kata. Kata techniques and forms are designed to show you how to combine all those kicks, strikes, blocks, etc. into a logical response to a specific attack or multiple attacks.

In all Martial Arts classes, training in focusing your mind to control panic and fear is an important component. The ability to properly access a situation and quickly determine the most appropriate action to take is a skill that must be honed and practiced like any other. Training and exercise in this kind of muscle memory is important.
